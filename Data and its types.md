**Data**

Data is raw information (numbers, text, images, audio, or video) that can be stored and processed. When analyzed, it becomes meaningful information.

**Types**

**Structured Data–** Organized in tables (e.g., Excel, SQL databases).

**Unstructured Data** – No fixed format (e.g., emails, images, videos, social media).

**Importance**

Data helps in Decision-making , Identifying trends , Improving systems , Predicting outcomes

**Usage**

Applied in business, healthcare, education, technology, and government.
