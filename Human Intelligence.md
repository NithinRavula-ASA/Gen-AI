**Human Intelligence**

Human intelligence refers to the natural mental ability of humans to learn, understand, reason, and solve problems. It enables individuals to adapt to new situations, make decisions, and apply knowledge effectively.

**Core Components**

Reasoning – Logical thinking and drawing conclusions

Learning – Acquiring knowledge from experience or study

Problem-Solving – Identifying and resolving challenges

Creativity – Generating new ideas and innovative approaches

Emotional Intelligence – Understanding and managing emotions

Adaptability – Adjusting to new environments or situations

**Importance**

Human intelligence is essential for:

Making informed decisions

Communicating effectively

Building relationships and societies

Innovating and advancing technology

Solving complex real-world problems

Application

It is used continuously in education, the workplace, social interactions, scientific research, and everyday life.
